About the Conference

The International Water Association (IWA) is an authoritative water industry association, dedicated to promoting the sustainable management of global water resources. Supported by IWA, the 2025 International Conference on Sustainable Urban Water Systems will be held under the theme "Resilient, Inclusive & Smart Water Futures".

The conference will focus on the challenges and innovative solutions for urban water systems amidst rapid urbanization, fostering interdisciplinary and cross-regional academic exchange and technical cooperation to contribute to achieving UN Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 6.

The College of Environmental Science and Engineering, Tongji University, in conjunction with IWA, is jointly organizing this high-level international conference, aiming to promote the transition of the water sector towards carbon neutrality, digitalization, and resource circularity.

Conference Topics

The conference will cover a wide range of topics related to sustainable urban water systems

01

Water Quality Security and Digital Technology

Advanced monitoring, AI-driven analytics, and digital transformation in water quality management

02

Green Water Treatment and Resource Recycling

Sustainable treatment technologies, resource recovery, and circular economy approaches

03

Water Environment Remediation and Healthy Cities

Urban water ecosystem restoration, public health, and creating liveable cities

04

Environmental System Engineering and Risk Control

System optimization, modeling, risk assessment for resilient water infrastructure

05

Industry-Academia-Research Forum

Bridging research and practice through collaborative innovation and technology transfer

06

Journal Forum of Energy & Environmental Sustainability

Carbon neutrality, energy efficiency, and renewable energy integration

07

Youth Student Forum

Platform for early-career researchers and students to present their work
