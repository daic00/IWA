Conference Registration

We warmly welcome scholars from both China and abroad to submit abstracts and attend the conference.

Registration Fees

Attendee Type Fee (CNY)
Non-IWA Member ¥2,000
IWA Member ¥1,800
Student ¥1,500

What's Included

The registration fee includes:

  • Access to all conference sessions (plenary, keynote, and parallel sessions)
  • Conference materials and program booklet
  • Lunch and refreshments during conference days
  • Welcome reception (December 18)
  • Conference proceedings
  • Certificate of attendance

Registration Methods

Online Registration

Register online via the conference website

Registration Website: Go to registration portal

On-site Registration

Registration will be available on-site during the conference

Location: Registration Desk, Conference Venue

Hours: December 18, 09:00 - 24:00

Important Information

Student Registration

  • Students must provide valid student ID or proof of enrollment
  • Student rate applies to full-time undergraduate and graduate students
  • Documentation will be verified during registration

IWA Membership

  • IWA members must provide membership number during registration
  • Not an IWA member? Join IWA to receive the discounted rate
  • Membership verification may be required

Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellations received before November 18, 2025: 80% refund
  • Cancellations received between November 18 - December 1, 2025: 50% refund
  • No refunds for cancellations after December 1, 2025
  • All refund requests must be submitted in writing to the organizing committee

Payment Methods

Details regarding payment methods will be provided once online registration opens.

Accepted Payment Methods

We will accept the following payment methods:

  • Bank transfer (details to be provided)
  • Credit card (through secure online payment gateway)
  • Cash (on-site registration only)
