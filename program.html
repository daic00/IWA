Session Topics

The conference features seven parallel sessions covering key aspects of sustainable urban water systems:

01

Water Quality Security and Digital Technology

Advanced monitoring, AI-driven analytics, and digital transformation in water quality management

02

Green Water Treatment and Resource Recycling

Sustainable treatment technologies, resource recovery, and circular economy approaches

03

Water Environment Remediation and Healthy Cities

Urban water ecosystem restoration, public health, and creating liveable cities

04

Environmental System Engineering and Risk Control

System optimization, modeling, risk assessment for resilient water infrastructure

05

Industry-Academia-Research Forum

Bridging research and practice through collaborative innovation and technology transfer

06

Energy & Environmental Sustainability

Carbon neutrality, energy efficiency, and renewable energy integration

07

Youth Student Forum

Platform for early-career researchers and students to present their work
