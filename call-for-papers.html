Submit Your Research

We invite scholars from China and abroad to submit abstracts and attend the 2025 International Conference on Sustainable Urban Water Systems. This is an excellent opportunity to share your research findings with leading experts in the field.

Conference Themes

The conference will focus on the following main themes:

Session 1: Water Quality Security and Digital Technology

  • Advanced water quality monitoring and sensing technologies
  • Artificial intelligence and machine learning in water management
  • Big data analytics for water security
  • Digital twins for water systems
  • Cybersecurity in water infrastructure

Session 2: Green Water Treatment and Resource Recycling

  • Nature-based solutions for water treatment
  • Resource recovery from wastewater
  • Circular economy in water sector
  • Energy-efficient treatment technologies
  • Nutrient recovery and reuse

Session 3: Water Environment Remediation and Healthy Cities

  • Urban water ecosystem restoration
  • Sponge city concepts and implementation
  • Water quality and public health
  • Green infrastructure for urban water management
  • Climate adaptation strategies

Session 4: Environmental System Engineering and Risk Control

  • System optimization and modeling
  • Risk assessment and management
  • Infrastructure resilience
  • Early warning systems
  • Emergency response planning

Session 5: Industry-Academia-Research Forum

  • Technology transfer and commercialization
  • Industry case studies
  • Public-private partnerships
  • Innovation in water utilities
  • Start-ups and entrepreneurship

Session 6: Journal Forum of Energy & Environmental Sustainability

  • Carbon neutrality in water sector
  • Renewable energy integration
  • Energy efficiency optimization
  • Water-energy nexus
  • Life cycle assessment

Session 7: Youth Student Forum

  • Early-career researcher presentations
  • Student research showcase
  • Graduate student perspectives
  • Academic career development
  • Mentorship and networking opportunities

Submission Guidelines

Abstract Format

  • Language: English
  • Length: 250-500 words
  • Include: Title, authors, affiliations, and keywords (3-5 keywords)
  • Structure: Background, Methods, Results, and Conclusions

Submission Method

Online submission via the conference website or on-site registration

Conference Website: Access the submission portal

Review Process

  • All abstracts will be peer-reviewed by the Programme Committee
  • Authors will be notified of acceptance status via email
  • Accepted abstracts will be presented as oral or poster presentations

Publication Opportunities

Selected papers will be invited for publication in:

Journal Forum of Energy & Environmental Sustainability

Special issue dedicated to conference proceedings

Conference Proceedings

All accepted abstracts will be published in the conference proceedings
