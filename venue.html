Booking Tips

  • Book your hotel as early as possible to secure the best rates
  • Consider hotels near metro stations for convenient access
  • Check if your hotel offers airport shuttle services
  • Verify that your hotel can accommodate international guests

Transportation

By Air

Shanghai Pudong International Airport (PVG)

The main international airport serving Shanghai

  • Location: 30 km east of downtown Shanghai
  • Transportation: Metro Line 2, Airport Bus, Taxi, Maglev Train
  • Travel Time to City: 45-60 minutes

Shanghai Hongqiao International Airport (SHA)

Domestic and regional international flights

  • Location: 13 km west of downtown Shanghai
  • Transportation: Metro Lines 2, 10, Airport Bus, Taxi
  • Travel Time to City: 30-40 minutes

Metro System

Shanghai's metro system is one of the world's largest and most efficient, with over 400 stations across 18 lines.

  • Operating hours: 5:30 AM - 11:00 PM (approximately)
  • Fares: ¥3-10 depending on distance
  • Purchase tickets at station vending machines or use Shanghai Metro app
  • Signs and announcements available in Chinese and English

Taxi

  • Taxis are readily available throughout the city
  • Base fare: ¥14 for first 3 km
  • Most drivers speak limited English - have your destination written in Chinese
  • Ride-hailing apps: Didi Chuxing (Chinese app), international cards may not work

Visa Information

Important Notice

International participants should check visa requirements for China well in advance of the conference.

  • Apply for your visa at least 4-6 weeks before travel
  • Invitation letters for visa applications can be requested from the organizing committee
  • Check with your local Chinese embassy or consulate for specific requirements
  • Some nationalities may be eligible for visa-free transit (up to 144 hours in Shanghai)
